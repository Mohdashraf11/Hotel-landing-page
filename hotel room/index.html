Room Type

Single occupancy

A single room in a hotel is a room designed for one person to sleep in and typically has a single bed. The bed is usually a queen or double bed

Family Room

The family room is designed to be a place where family and guests gather for group recreation like talking, reading, watching TV, and other family activities.

Duplex Room

A duplex room in a hotel is a large, luxurious room with two floors connected by an internal staircase. The separate sitting and sleeping areas make duplex rooms ideal for large families and groups.
